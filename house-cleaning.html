House Cleaning

When we clean, we do it right. Here is the list of what is included in a regular clean:

  • Bathrooms - Scrub shower, tub, toilet, sinks, clean mirrors, dust, wipe down cabinets, vacuum and mop
  • Kitchen - clean and polish appliances, clean counters, wipe down cabinets*, dust, vacuum and mop
  • Bedrooms - polish mirrors, change sheets if desired
  • General household - general dusting, dusting doorframes and baseboards*, polish glass surfaces and doors, vacuum, mop hard floors
  • Extras (upon request) - cleaning inside refrigerator, inside cabinets
